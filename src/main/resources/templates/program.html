About the Music Club

Come to the Music Club for a wonderful music filled experience.

In the Music Club you can play and create music with your friends, learn how to play an instrument or learn about the music business.

Community Based Teaching

In the Music Club there isn't solely one or two teachers, rather an atmosphere and environment.

In this club the students are broken up into groups based on what they want to learn, and the teach and exchange knowledge with each other.

Being taught by your peers offers and fun learning enriched environment filled with understanding and patience for your learning and craft.

What You'll Learn

Sheet Music:Learn to read sheet music DAW Digital Audio Workstations: Fl Studio, Ableton, Reason Songwriting: Learn song strucutres and write lyrics Music Business: Learn to about the Music Industry Learn Instruments of Choice: A safe space to learn and new instrument with friends

Learning Groups

Sheet Music Group

Students group together to learn how to read sheet music.

DAW Group

Students will learn how to use various Digital Audio Workstations such as FL Studio, ProTools, Ableton, Reason and/or Logic.

Songwriting Group

Students will learn to writing songs and learn about different song structures.

Music Business

Students will learn about the ins and outs of the music industry and business.

Learn Instruments of Choice

Students will work together to teach other the instruments that the want to learn.
